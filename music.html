The Roland electric piano and Pianoteq are the most important parts of my compositions. On the piano, I compose the pieces and then transcribe them into MIDI. Finally I use Pianoteq for debugging before publishing my music.


Nights and Dreams

  • Composer: Reon Chen, 2022.
  • Introduction: The canvas uses the flickering of lights to imply the flickering dreams. The flickering of the lights represents the volume of the music. I composed the song using piano, in which the introductory part is calm, presenting the serenity of the night, and the climax symbolizes people going to dreamland deep in their minds.

Say Goodbye to You

  • Composer: Reon Chen, 2022.
  • Introduction: This piece is modeled after some classical piano elements, making this piece with some vintage style. Therefore, for the design of the picture, I used a minimalist design, using a black background and white lines to simulate a vintage effect.

Ripple

  • Composer: Reon Chen, 2020.
  • Introduction: This work depicts the feeling of ripples in my mind. On the canvas, I used ripples to construct the feeling of watergou waves, which might be different when viewed on a fine beach. For the composition of the piece, I used a triplet-based main melody and repeated the passage to construct the melody of the water waves.
